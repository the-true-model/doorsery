Your Trusted Door Supply Partner

Doorsery, operated under Unimirror Enterprises, was founded in 2019 with a vision to revolutionize fresh produce accessibility in tier-2 cities across India. What began as an online grocery platform has evolved into a robust B2B supply chain solution.

Our journey started by addressing a critical gap in the market-connecting local retailers directly with producers and wholesalers in the fresh produce sector. By eliminating intermediaries and streamlining logistics, we enabled tier-2 city retailers to access quality fruits and vegetables at competitive prices while ensuring producers received fair value for their goods.

Today, Doorsery has scaled into a comprehensive B2B supply chain platform, serving major Indian retailers across the country. Our operations have a particularly strong presence in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, where we have established deep relationships with both suppliers and retail partners.

Through technology-driven logistics and strategic partnerships, Doorsery continues to bridge the gap between farm and retail, ensuring freshness, efficiency, and reliability in India's fresh produce supply chain.
